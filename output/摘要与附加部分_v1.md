# 摘要与附加部分

## 摘要

等离子体作为一种典型的色散介质，其非线性电磁特性在微波频段会对穿透其中的电磁波产生强烈的色散调制作用。传统的线性调频连续波（LFMCW）雷达在诊断等离子体时，通常基于全频段恒定频差的假设。然而，在强色散区域（尤其是接近等离子体频率处），群时延的非线性变化将导致差频信号的频谱严重散焦，传统的高分辨测距与参数反演方法随之失效。为了解决强色散条件下的诊断难题，本文突破了传统全频段带宽耦合带来的适用性边界，提出了一种基于滑动时频特征提取与加权非线性反演（MCMC贝叶斯）的色散等离子体诊断新方法。本文的主要研究工作和创新点如下：

（1）系统剖析了色散效应对 LFMCW 差频信号的调制机理，建立了相关的物理模型。通过推导弹介电常数及群时延表达式，定义了衡量色散强度的非线性度因子 $\eta$，并推导出传统 FFT 方法失效的工程适用性判据 $B \cdot \eta \cdot \tau_0 \le 1$。揭示了时变时延如何导致差频信号相位畸变和频谱散焦，从物理层面上论证了传统全频段处理方法的失效机理。

（2）提出了一种应对强色散信号的高分辨率特征提取策略。鉴于碰撞频率 $\nu_e$ 对群时延的贡献仅为二阶微扰，本文在物理约束下论证了参数降维的可行性方案。通过引入滑动窗口机制进行时频解耦，并结合 MDL 准则进行信源估计和 ESPRIT 算法实现超分辨频率提取，有效抑制了频谱散焦效应，成功提取出精确的高分辨率时延轨迹。

（3）构建了基于 Metropolis-Hastings (MCMC) 算法的贝叶斯参数反演模型以解决参数映射的多解性问题。在反演策略上，将碰撞频率 $\nu_e$ 设定为常数，仅对电子密度 $n_e$ 进行单一参数的降维反演。通过联合 Drude 等离子体模型与加权似然代价函数，实现了从定量的时延特征到核心等离子体参数的高精度映射，并在不同信噪比条件下进行了鲁棒性仿真测试与不确定性量化。

（4）设计了宽带 LFMCW 诊断系统，并利用微波带通滤波器完成了色散等效的实验验证。论证了带通滤波器与 Drude/Lorentz 色散模型在群时延变化上的物理等效机理，提取了等效介质的时延轨迹，成功以真实的硬件系统验证了本文提出的特征提取与反演算法在实际工程层面的有效性和普适性。

本文的研究成果不仅为复杂等离子体环境的高精度微波诊断提供了一套完整的理论框架和算法体系，同时也为其他强色散介质的 LFMCW 参数分析与等效特征评估提供了重要的理论参考和技术支撑。

**关键词**：等离子体诊断；LFMCW雷达；色散效应；时延轨迹；贝叶斯反演

## Abstract

As a typical dispersive medium, plasma exhibits nonlinear electromagnetic characteristics that strongly modulate electromagnetic waves penetrating through it in the microwave band. Traditional Linear Frequency Modulated Continuous Wave (LFMCW) radars, when diagnosing plasma, usually rely on the assumption of a constant frequency difference across the entire frequency band. However, in strongly dispersive regions (especially near the plasma frequency), the nonlinear variation of group delay leads to severe spectral defocusing of the beat signal, rendering traditional high-resolution ranging and parameter inversion methods ineffective. To overcome the diagnostic challenges under strong dispersion conditions, this thesis breaks through the applicability boundaries imposed by traditional full-band bandwidth coupling and proposes a novel dispersive plasma diagnostic method based on sliding time-frequency feature extraction and weighted nonlinear Bayesian inversion (MCMC). The main research work and innovations of this thesis are as follows:

(1) The modulation mechanism of dispersion effects on the LFMCW beat signal is systematically analyzed, and related physical models are established. By deriving the complex permittivity and group delay expressions, a nonlinearity factor $\eta$ measuring the dispersion strength is defined, and the engineering applicability criterion $B \cdot \eta \cdot \tau_0 \le 1$ for the failure of the traditional FFT method is derived. It reveals how time-varying delay causes phase distortion and spectral defocusing in the beat signal, physically verifying the failure mechanism of traditional full-band processing methods.

(2) A high-resolution feature extraction strategy for strongly dispersive signals is proposed. Given that the contribution of the collision frequency $\nu_e$ to the group delay is only a second-order perturbation, the feasibility of parameter dimensionality reduction is demonstrated under physical constraints. By introducing a sliding window mechanism for time-frequency decoupling, combined with the MDL criterion for source estimation and the ESPRIT algorithm for super-resolution frequency extraction, the spectral defocusing effect is effectively suppressed, successfully extracting precise high-resolution delay trajectories.

(3) A Bayesian parameter inversion model based on the Metropolis-Hastings (MCMC) algorithm is constructed to solve the multi-solution problem in parameter mapping. In the inversion strategy, the collision frequency $\nu_e$ is fixed as a constant, and only a single-parameter dimension reduction inversion for the electron density $n_e$ is performed. By combining the Drude plasma model with a weighted likelihood cost function, high-precision mapping from the delay trajectory to the core plasma parameters is achieved, and robustness simulation testing and uncertainty quantification are conducted under different signal-to-noise ratio conditions.

(4) A broadband LFMCW diagnostic system is designed, and the experimental verification of dispersion equivalence is completed using microwave bandpass filters. The physical equivalent mechanism of group delay variation between bandpass filters and Drude/Lorentz dispersion models is demonstrated. The delay trajectories of the equivalent medium are extracted, successfully verifying the effectiveness and universality of the feature extraction and inversion algorithms proposed in this thesis at the practical engineering level using a real hardware system.

The research outcomes of this thesis not only provide a complete theoretical framework and algorithm system for high-precision microwave diagnostics in complex plasma environments but also offer important theoretical references and technical support for LFMCW parameter analysis and equivalent feature evaluation in other strongly dispersive media.

**Keywords**: Plasma diagnostics; LFMCW radar; Dispersion effect; Delay trajectory; Bayesian inversion

## 总结与展望

### 1. 全文工作总结
本文围绕色散等离子体环境下的微波诊断难题，通过理论推导、算法建模、仿真分析与等效实验验证，形成了一套完整的 LFMCW 强色散信号处理与参数反演体系。首先，建立了色散信道的物理模型，揭示了群时延随频率非线性变化导致 LFMCW 差频信号频谱散焦的机理，并推导了传统全频段分析方法的工程失效边界；其次，利用碰撞频率的二阶微扰特性进行降维，提出了基于滑动窗口和 ESPRIT 算法的高分辨率时延轨迹特征提取策略；随后，应对单点非线性映射带来的多解性挑战，构建了基于 MCMC 算法的贝叶斯参数反演模型，实现了电子密度的稳健观测；最后，借助微波带通滤波器完成了微波波段色散等效实验，验证了算法与测试系统的工程可行性与普适性。

### 2. 下一步工作展望
尽管本文在色散等离子体诊断算法与物理等效上取得了阶段性成果，但在未来的研究中仍有进一步拓展的空间：
（1）**动态与非均匀等离子体建模**：引入等离子体密度随空间梯度变化（非均匀分布）与时变物理流场的复杂测试条件，进一步提升算法在真实高超声速飞行器等恶劣通信环境中的适应极限。
（2）**实时信号处理与硬件部署**：目前的特征降维与 MCMC 反演仍侧重于高精度的离线计算，未来可通过 FPGA 等平台硬件加速特征参数并行提取，推进诊断算法与微波雷达硬件的实时化集成。
（3）**实测等离子体系统的联合诊断**：从微波硬件等效测试迈向真实的激波管或气体放电等离子体环境实验，验证理论模型在高温真空电离状态下的终端反演精度。
